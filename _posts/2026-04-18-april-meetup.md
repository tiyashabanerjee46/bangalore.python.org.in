The April Bangpypers meetup was primarily themed around AI in Production and Security in Supply chain got Python in the era of AI, where when we see a lot of vibecoded application going to production how we can ensure quality and security around it, event was hosted by [Cyware Labs](https://www.cyware.com/) in HSR Layout.

[Jagan PS](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jagan-ps-130908186/) kicked off the session by giving insights from his personal experience on working with production grade RAG system and how he evolved his system from RAG to Agentic RAG and then recently Vectorless RAG where he demostrated the performance boost and retriving logical chunks from a given data source.

![RAG to Vectorless RAG](https://secure.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/4/f/f/7/highres_533780471.webp "RAG to Vectorless RAG")

[Tushar Sadhwani](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tusharsadhwani/) talked about the risks related .pth files and how it was recently exploited in recent litellm incident and how one can protect themselves from such attack, he also presented a PEP about `site.toml` that is proposed to remove the use of .pth file altogether, checkout his talk here : https://github.com/tusharsadhwani/pthtalk

![Secure Supply Chain for Python in the ERA of AI](https://secure.meetupstatic.com/photos/event/4/f/f/3/highres_533780467.webp "Secure Supply Chain for Python in the ERA of AI")

Next we received three Lightning talks on the spot,

1. Robort Farmwork in Python by [Gopalakrishnan N](https://www.linkedin.com/in/gopalngk/) talking about how the framework to follow on how to use robotic framework with python application.
2. [Leafy Extension](https://chrome-stats.com/d/doohbmfpjanoimbigjbjocanpenbfkkh) by karan mannan on how he built it and is trying to make it available in chrome store/
3. [Github Action Security - Actsense](https://kumarashwin.com/projects/actsense/) by Ashwin Kumar showcased a project to detect vulnerabilities and analyze dependencies for securing projects in githuba actions.
